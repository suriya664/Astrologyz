Aries

March 21 - April 19

Traits & Characteristics

Aries are known for their bold, ambitious, and energetic nature. As the first sign of the zodiac, they are natural leaders who aren't afraid to take initiative. They possess an adventurous spirit and are always ready for new challenges.

Element: Fire
Ruling Planet: Mars
Quality: Cardinal

Love & Relationships

In love, Aries are passionate and direct. They value honesty and aren't afraid to express their feelings. They seek partners who can match their energy and share their adventurous spirit. Aries are loyal and protective of those they care about.

Best Matches: Leo, Sagittarius, Gemini
Compatible: Aquarius, Libra

Career & Finance

Aries excel in leadership roles and competitive environments. They thrive in careers that allow them to take initiative and work independently. Their natural confidence makes them excellent entrepreneurs, athletes, and managers.

Ideal Careers: Entrepreneur, Athlete, Manager, Sales
Financial Style: Impulsive but strategic

Health & Wellness

Aries rule the head and face, so they should pay attention to headaches and stress. Regular exercise is essential for maintaining their high energy levels. They benefit from activities that challenge them physically and mentally.

Health Focus: Head, face, stress management
Exercise: High-intensity, competitive sports
